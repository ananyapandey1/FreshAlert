Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshAlert. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you use our web application and tells you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Identity & Account Data: Your email address and a cryptographically hashed version of your password.
  • Inventory Data: Information about the food products you track, including product names, photos (if uploaded), and expiry dates.
  • Google User Data: If you choose to sync with Google Calendar, we receive authorization tokens via Google OAuth.

3. Limited Use Disclosure (Google User Data)

FreshAlert's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, for Google Calendar Synchronization:

  • FreshAlert requests access to the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events scope.
  • We only use this permission to create new calendar events corresponding to your food expiry dates and a 7-day prior warning.
  • We do not read your existing calendar events, do not delete other events, and do not share your calendar data with any third parties.
  • You can unlink your Google account at any time via the Settings menu.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to provide our food waste reduction service, maintain your inventory list, and send you automated alerts via Google Calendar.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

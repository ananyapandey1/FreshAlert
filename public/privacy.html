Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshAlert. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our web application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address and encrypted password.
  • Inventory Data: Names and expiry dates of products you scan or enter.
  • Google OAuth Data: If you connect your Google Calendar, we store an access token to create calendar events on your behalf. We do NOT read your other calendar events.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the core FreshAlert service (tracking expiry dates).
  • Sync expiry dates to your Google Calendar (if enabled).
  • Send notification alerts.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon and Vercel.

5. Your Rights

You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings menu or by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact the FreshAlert team.
